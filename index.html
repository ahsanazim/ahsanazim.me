About me

My name is Ahsan Azim!

I'm "from" Lahore, Pakistan, although my dad's job has meant that I've lived variously in New York, the Netherlands, and (kind of) Italy.

Currently, I'm a sophomore at Dartmouth College, in Hanover, New Hampshire. I'm studying Computer Science, Math, and Government. I really enjoy each of those subjects, and plan for a career that somehow mixes at least two of them

If you want to get specific, then my academic interests are as follows:

  • Computer Science : Machine Learning, Data Analysis, NLP, iOS App Development
  • Mathematics : Statistics, Linear Algebra, Pure Mathematics
  • Government : Comparative Politics
